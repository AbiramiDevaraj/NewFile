About

Dr. John W. Cornelius

I am a psychotherapist who works with adults, children, families, adolescents and couples. My private practice is in Dupont Circle, Washington, D.C. and Chevy Chase, MD. My training and experience allow me to assist clients who need help with current crisis situations and healing from painful childhood experiences. I help individuals learn skills to improve their quality of contact in relationships and to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression.
